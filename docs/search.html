Internet Search

10 years ago, there was no way to find out if any webpages except Mosaic, Internet Explorer, there was no way to search the web and it was a big problem. Companies that entered the search business were big companies like Yahoo, even the big player couldn’t crack code of proper develop search tools for people to use web easily.

All of us may be thinking the next big idea will come from Apple, Microsoft maybe some other big high-tech company but we had to go to one place for the next big invention, which is Stanford University. Two graduate students name Jerry Yang and David filo were left left alone while their professor was on one-year Sabbatical leave. They were goofing around with a technology which will later turn to be a traffic search engine Yahoo.

Yahoo was a great idea, but all great idea don’t make great business because it requires money and as most of us know that Silicon Valley is full of it. One of the successful Venture Capitalist firm is Sequoia Capital, its most famous partner Michael Mortiz decided to pay meet the people that created Yahoo. What he saw in the was upsetting for him as he describe it as “Every mom biggest nightmare”. The Yahoo company was ran by two people with housekeeping in place, everything was mess. Michael was hesitant about Yahoo after that.

In 2007, search company such Yahoo and google generated billions in revenue, making them the envy of entrepreneur everywhere.

On the other hand, no one really used it for business or commerce, some even thought it was evil to do business online. Until, Adverting took over the web, Yahoo had figure out a way to make money but that will cause them discomfort because at first they were worried that this move might push away their customers but later realized that it was the only option to survive.

In 1995, Yahoo started taking banner ads with their fingers crossed, for the first time Yahoo had shown that there was a way to make money over the web. This was a crucial moment because web boom has begun.

In 1996, Yahoo faced a number of challenges as it had grown competitors breathing under their neck, Excited was the most noticed competitor of Yahoo because their technology was more sophisticated and complex in comparison to Yahoo.

At this stage, Internet was exploding Yahoo, Excited, all companies were turning their search page into portal. All companies were drifted from the main idea was to help people search for what they are looking for, in other words, the search company no longer cared about search. This made the world hungry for better way from searching the web.

This resulted in a lawsuit between Google and Bill gross but it was settled and both parties were happy. As a result of the new addition to Google, became the search engine marketing tool.

This IPO was talk on the world at the time. Google model was “Don’t be Evil”. Later Google launched Google Maps, docs, mail, calendar, blogger, Picasa, Youtube. Google was now dominating the world.
